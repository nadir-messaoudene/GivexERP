Odoo Schedule Activity Assign to Multiple Users app helps to allow Multiple-Mass User at a same time in schedule activity a system will be split activity by allow and assigned users.

Assign Multiple Users at the Same Time

Odoo Assign Schedule Activity to Multiple Users to a particular record at the same time in Sales & CRM Modules.

Users can See Own Scheduled Activity after they Log-in in Sales

Every user can check his/her activity in their particular records in Sales after they are assigned to the activity.

Users can See Own Scheduled Activity after they Log-in in CRM

Every user can check his/her activity in their particular records in CRM after they are assigned to the activity.

Description

Create a Sales Quotation/Order, and there you will see a Scheduled Activity button on the Chatter Box of that Quotation/Order.


On clicking of Scheduled Activity button, you can see a Assign Multi Users field where you can assign as many users as you want.



After clicking on Schedule button, all the users mentioned on Assign Multi Users field will be notified of the activity on that Quotation/Order, when they Log-in.



For Example, Demo User will be able to see his activity whenever he access his Quotation/Order.


Similarly, create a CRM Pipeline, and there you will see a Scheduled Activity button on the Chatter Box of that Pipeline Order.


On clicking of Scheduled Activity button, you can see a Assign Multi Users field where you can assign as many users as you want.


After clicking on Schedule button, all the users mentioned on Assign Multi Users field will be notified of the activity on that Pipeline order, when they Log-in.



For Example, Demo User will be able to see his activity whenever he access his Pipeline Order.



An User named Hardeep will be notified of his activity when he access his Pipeline Order.
